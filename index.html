Why this site

I travel because I cannot help it. I write because memory fades, and a place visited but not told is a place half-lost. This is my notebook, made public — not a guide, not a brochure: a journal.

Photos and routes are sketches, not prescriptions. If something here pulls you out of your chair, I have done my job.
