## Things that go wrong (and what to do)

**Vehicle breakdown.** Carry a basic toolkit, jump leads, tow rope. The local guide handles most field repairs.

**Tyre punctures.** Expect 2–4 over the trip. Repair at the next town. Carry a 12 V air compressor.

**Border delays.** Build a 2-hour buffer into any day with a border crossing.

**Park camps full.** Confirmed bookings essential. Have a fallback bushcamp option.

**Police "fines".** Smile, comply, negotiate quietly. $5–10 USD usually settles speed traps. Never hand over your original passport.

**Rental car company adding charges after return.** Photograph the vehicle inside and out at pickup AND drop-off. Get a written "no damage" report.

**Running out of cash.** ATMs unreliable. Carry at least 30 % more cash than projected.

**Someone needs to fly home.** Closest international airport: XXX.
